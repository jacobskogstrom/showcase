Terms of Service.

Draft v0.1 · 2026-07-06 · plain-language on purpose

1. What you're buying

A personal AI operator: a private, always-on assistant that acts on your behalf within the abilities ("scoops") you enable. It is a service, not software you install. We provision it, keep it running, and keep it private.

2. It acts only where you let it

  • Each ability is opt-in. No enabled ability, no access — an operator without the mail scoop cannot read mail.
  • Outbound actions (emails, invites, posts) are previewed to you first, until you explicitly loosen that per ability.
  • You can pause everything with one message ("pause my operator") and resume the same way.

3. Privacy commitment

  • Your operator runs in an isolated space. No data is shared between customers.
  • Your data is not used to train models — ours or anyone else's.
  • Credentials are encrypted at rest. You can rotate or revoke them any time.
  • Everything your operator remembers is stored as readable files. You can export or delete your full memory whenever you want, and it goes with you if you leave.
  • Service operated from Sweden under EU data-protection rules.

4. Payment & cancellation

  • Monthly subscription, charged in advance. No binding period.
  • Change scoops or plans any time; price changes apply from the next month.
  • Cancel by chat or account page. Your memory export link stays live for 30 days after cancellation, then everything is destroyed.

5. Fair and safe use

  • Your operator won't impersonate you deceptively, break laws, or act against the platform rules of connected services.
  • Rate limits protect you and the service; sustained heavy automation may need a bigger plan.
  • We may refuse or unwind actions that look like account takeover or abuse — that protection is a feature.

6. What we don't promise

  • An operator is capable but not infallible. Review anything high-stakes before it goes out — it will insist on this by default.
  • No financial, legal or medical advice; it will tell you to see a professional instead.

7. Liability

Capped at the fees you paid in the last 12 months. We carry responsibility for keeping the service secure and private; you carry responsibility for the instructions you give it.
