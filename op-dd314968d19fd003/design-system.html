Design system

One system, seven pages.

Tokens and components behind the Operator concept site. Everything below is live CSS from operator.css — feed this page to Claude Design as the visual contract.

Principles

Apple-grade restraint: one accent color, generous whitespace, hairline dividers, big confident type. Cards carry depth with soft double shadows, never borders. Dark bands are reserved for the two moments of drama (the assistant-vs-employee duel, the final CTA). Motion is one pattern only: fade-up on scroll. Price is always honest — the configurator tells you when a plan beats your mix.
